About Us

HKR BUNDLE is an thrift store. For now, we only have one outlet at Alor Setar. We offer a selection of Knitwear, Ladies Pants and Skirt. You can find and buy high quality secondhand clothes and vintage at HKR Bundle. 

Vision

Make the bundle brands as the first choice to people

Mission

 Be different through our branding to stay ahead of fashion trends

Objective

1. To make customers can save the budget when buying the clothes.
2. To avoid wasting a lot of clothes.
3. To help customers try the unique design.
